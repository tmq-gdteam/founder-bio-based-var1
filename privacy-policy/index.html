Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit this website. This is a personal founder website and publishing platform. The site exists to share writing, a project archive, current work, and ways to get in touch.

You provide this information voluntarily, and only when you choose to use those features. If you do not use a form or signup feature, you are not asked to provide personal information to read the site.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, this site may receive standard technical information that your browser sends when you visit, such as your device type, browser type, general location inferred from your network, and the pages you view. Information is used to operate and maintain the website, to respond to messages you send, to deliver writing or updates you have asked to receive, and to understand and improve how the site is used. Information is not sold.

Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the website, such as hosting, email delivery, or analytics providers, and only to the extent needed for those services. Information may also be shared where required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of the site or its visitors.

This website may rely on third-party services for hosting, analytics, email delivery, or links to external sites and projects. Those services handle information under their own privacy policies. Following an external link means the destination site's own policies apply.

Data protection

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the information handled through this site. No method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and no absolute guarantee of security is made.

Your choices

You can choose not to submit information through any form, and you can unsubscribe from any optional updates using the method provided in those messages. You can also adjust your browser settings to limit or block cookies, though some features may not work as intended if you do.

Children's privacy
