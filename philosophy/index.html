Worldview

Philosophy

Humans are strongest at creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking. Machines are strongest at repetition, information organization, and precision.

Thesis

Useful systems respect the difference.

The goal is not to make every tool feel intelligent. The goal is to build systems that let people apply judgment while machines handle structure, repetition, and exactness.

This is a practical stance, not a slogan. It asks what work should become easier, what should stay in human hands, and what the system should make clear at each step.

Human vs Machine

Complementary strengths, not a contest.

Human strengths

together

Useful technology

Serious systems do not need hype language.

The useful-technology stance is simple: be specific, stay grounded, and build for the work people actually need to do.
